# Accountability Jar

Because accountability needs consequences. $1 per missed goal. May this jar stay empty.

## What it is

A small web app that tracks penalty dollars for missed commitments. Every time someone doesn't do what they said they would, they owe $1 into the jar. The jar fills up visually as the total grows, and a log tracks every entry with a timestamp.

## How to use it

1. Open `index.html` in a browser, or visit the hosted URL
2. When someone misses a commitment, click their button to add $1
3. A coin drops into the jar and the log records the entry
4. At the end of each meeting, note the totals and decide what the money goes toward

## Features

- Visual jar that fills up with coins as the total grows
- Separate tracking per person
- Running total
- Timestamped log of every entry (up to 20 shown)
- Reset button to clear the jar at the start of a new period

## Context

This jar is part of an accountability system for two people working toward a shared goal. Regular check-ins, shared commitments, and — when needed — a dollar in the jar.
